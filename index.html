About Us

Transforming Outdoor Spaces Across Lafayette

G&C Landscaping Services is a start-up company dedicated to the design, transformation, and comprehensive maintenance of residential and commercial green spaces. Born from the dream and technical experience of two friends and brothers, our philosophy is based on delivering the highest quality and effort in every project. We specialize in landscaping, lawn care, and irrigation systems, always working under the values of simplicity, transparency, and fair prices. Our absolute priority is to earn our clients' trust, guaranteeing their complete satisfaction.
